About Me

I’m a technical operator and builder with experience across enterprise IT support, browser-based software projects, data experimentation, and AI-assisted workflow design.

My background started in hands-on support and operations: device deployment, identity migration support, infrastructure troubleshooting, user communication, office technology, and safety-critical team supervision. That experience shaped how I work now. I care about whether something works in the real environment, whether the process can be repeated, and whether the result can be checked.

My projects focus on practical tools and evidence-driven work. I’ve built job-search workflow systems, a browser-based UML diagramming tool, data mining experiments, interactive web apps, and AI-assisted project workflows. I use tools like Codex and Claude to move faster, but I stay honest about the limits: source files, live endpoints, generated output, browser behavior, and clear acceptance criteria all matter.

I bring a careful, direct, and human style to technical work. I like clarity, I care about people using the thing, and I’m comfortable being the person who asks what is actually true before the team keeps moving. Outside of work, I stay close to creative projects, surfing, skateboarding, and design.
